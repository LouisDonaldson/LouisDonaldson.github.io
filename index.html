About us

Bywater is a haven for wildlife and is set on the edge of the small village of Newport at the foot of the Yorkshire Wolds. Comprising of a luxury log cabin, overlooking a mature three acre lake set within nine acres of landscaped gardens, woodland and meadowland.

As the occupier of the cabin you will have exclusive use of this beautiful lake, making Bywater perfect for a tranquil getaway or a fisherman's heaven. As well as an array of wildlife around the lake, there is an excellent stock of mirror carp, common carp, bream tench, rudd, roach, perch, eels pike and ghost carp.

Bywater Holiday Lodge

Set at the edge of a beautiful lake surrounded by 9 acres of landscaped garden, woodland and meadowland. Bywater Lake is over 100 years old and has matured into a beautiful habitat for an array of wildlife and fish.

The lodge is finished to a very high standard with luxury fixtures and fine furnishings to make your stay very comfortable. It consists of open plan living and dining areas with a 42" LCD T.V. Including freeview with a modern kitchen including dishwasher, cooker and hob, fridge with icebox and microwave.

There are two bedrooms made up by one king-size room with en suite bathroom and large shower, and one twin bedroom with the use of an additional family bathroom.

The large veranda includes a hot tub overlooking a patio barbecue area (barbecue provided).

If you would like to see more pictures of the lodge and its beautiful surroundings then please take a look at our Gallery page.

Please note: This is an adult only site (also we do not accept all Male/Female parties or under 25's). There will be an extra £20 charge for the Hot Tub.

Fishing at Bywater

The fishing on the lake is exclusive for the residents of the log cabin and unhooking mats and landing nets are provided. There is also a building with a bait fridge and storage for fishing tackle.

Bywater Lake was formed well over 100 years ago from the extraction of clay for brick and tile making industry. Since then it has transformed into a beautiful lake which is now well stocked mixed coarse lake and home to some specimen carp up to 28lbs, pike up to 25 lbs, bream up to 11 lbs, eels up to 6 lbs!

If you are coming here to fish for the Carp please note it is not a Carp runs water, these beautiful Carp do not give themselves up easily and will test your skill and water craft, but is well worth the effort when you land one.

There are 8 swims to choose from set around the lake.
