Rider Training

We offer a variety of courses to meet your motorbike training needs, including:

  • Compulsory Basic Training (CBT): The foundational course for all new riders, ensuring you have the basic skills and knowledge to ride safely on the road.
  • A1 License: For riders aged 17 and above, allowing you to ride light motorcycles up to 125cc.
  • A2 License: For riders aged 19 and above, enabling you to ride motorcycles with a power output up to 35kW
  • A License: The unrestricted license for riders aged 24 and above (or 21 and above with progressive access), allowing you to ride any motorcycle.
  • Enhanced Rider Scheme (ERS): Aimed at improving your riding skills and safety, suitable for riders of all levels.
  • Direct Access Scheme (DAS): For those aged 24 and above, providing direct access to larger motorcycles without the need for progressive licensing.
Our comprehensive training programs are designed to ensure you gain the necessary skills and confidence to ride safely and legally on the road.

CBT's

There are 5 elements that make up the CBT.

  1. Introduction and Purpose of the CBT:
    • Licensing and eyesight check required.
    • Topics: helmets, appropriate clothing, and legal requirements.
  2. Practical On-Site Training
    • Introduction to motorbike controls, safety checks, stands, wheeling, and engine operation.
  3. Practical On-Site Riding
    • Off-road riding: moving off, stopping, straight-line riding, slow riding, steering manoeuvres, figure of 8, U-turns, braking, and gear changes.
  4. Practical On-Road Training
    • Theory session: highway code, road safety, legal requirements, road positioning, and attitude.
  5. Practical On-Road Riding
    • Minimum of two hours on-road riding with a trainer, handling various situations and hazards.

Driver Training

We currently offer driving lessons in both Kingston upon Hull and Lincoln. Our team of highly qualified instructors, all graded A by the DVSA, brings a wealth of experience with a minimum of 15 years in driving tuition. We are proud to offer both male and female instructors to ensure you feel comfortable and supported throughout your learning journey.

Our training programs are fully customizable to meet your individual needs and learning pace. Whether you prefer to spread your lessons over a longer period or wish to complete your training quickly, we can accommodate your schedule and preferences. Our goal is to provide a supportive and effective learning environment, helping you become a confident and safe driver.

In addition to standard driving lessons, we offer a range of services to enhance your driving skills and knowledge:

  • Under 17's Crash Course: This training course is designed to give 15 to 16 year olds an opportunity to learn the skills needed to drive.
  • Refresher Courses: Ideal for those who haven't driven in a while and need to regain confidence on the road.
  • Pass Plus Scheme: Additional training to improve your driving skills and safety, often leading to insurance discounts.
  • Motorway Driving Lessons: Specialized training for navigating motorways safely and confidently.
  • Night Driving Lessons: Focused on improving your skills and confidence when driving in low-light conditions.
  • Mock Driving Tests: Simulated driving tests to help you prepare for the real thing and identify areas for improvement.
  • Theory Test Preparation: Comprehensive support and resources to help you pass your theory test.
We are committed to providing high-quality, personalized driving instruction that meets your unique needs and helps you achieve your driving goals.

Instructor Training

Why pay thousands of pounds when you can learn with us on a flexible pay-as-you-go basis? At our training school, we offer an affordable and convenient path to becoming a qualified driving instructor.

Our team of professional grade A instructors are dedicated to providing personalised training tailored to meet your individual needs. With their extensive experience and expertise, they will guide you through every step of the process, ensuring you gain the skills and confidence required to succeed.

  1. Theory:
    We provide a comprehensive learning app that includes all the latest DVSA questions and answers, as well as hazard perception videos required for the ADI theory test. This app allows you to learn all the necessary topics, practice sections you are unsure of, and take mock theory tests to prepare you thoroughly. You can study independently at no cost, but we also offer training support if needed to ensure you are fully prepared.
  2. Driving:
    Our in-car training is conducted in our instructor vehicles, helping you achieve the driving proficiency required to pass your Part 2 test. You can also practice these skills in your own time, maintaining a high standard of driving essential for your new career. Once you have passed Part 1, you can apply for Part 2. You have the option to take the test in our vehicle or your own, provided it meets the test requirements.
  3. Instruction:
    To develop your instructional skills in a safe and supportive environment, we start your training with you teaching your trainer. Our instructor will simulate the role of a learner driver, allowing you to build essential teaching skills from the outset. Once you feel confident, you can begin instructing real students with genuine training needs. After passing Part 2, you can apply for your Part 3 qualifying exam as soon as you are ready. Again, you can use either our vehicle or your own for this test.
