MSM Rider Driver Training

Welcome, we are your trusted family-owned motorbike and car training school. With a passion for driving and a commitment to safety, we have been proudly serving our community for over 20 years.

At MSM, we believe that learning to drive should be a fun, safe, and an empowering experience. Our team of experienced instructors are dedicated to providing personalised training that caters to the unique needs of each student. Whether you're a first-time driver, looking to enhance your skills or become an instructor, we are here to guide you every step of the way.

Our Training Services

Riding

We offer a comprehensive range of training programs to meet all your motorbike licensing needs. Our services include Compulsory Basic Training (CBT), A1, A2, Direct Access Scheme (DAS) or the Enhanced Rider Scheme (ERS). Additionally, we provide both off-road and on-road training to ensure you are fully prepared for any riding conditions.

Book your CBT now Find out more

Driving

We offer driving lessons to ensure you pass your driving test with confidence. Additionally, we provide Pass Plus schemes to further enhance your driving skills and safety on the road. Our experienced, grade A instructors provide personalized training tailored to your individual needs, covering all aspects of driving from basic skills to advanced techniques.

Find out more

Instructing

We offer driving instructor training on a flexible pay-as-you-go basis. Our comprehensive program covers all aspects of becoming a qualified driving instructor, including theoretical knowledge, practical driving skills, and instructional techniques. This ensures you are fully equipped to teach and guide new drivers with confidence and expertise.
