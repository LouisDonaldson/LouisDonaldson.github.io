What we provide

Rider Training

We offer a comprehensive range of training programs to meet all your motorbike licensing needs. Our services include Compulsory Basic Training (CBT) and various license categories such as A1, A2 or Direct Access Scheme (DAS). Additionally, we provide both off-road and on-road training to ensure you are fully prepared for any riding conditions.

Find out more

Driver Training

We offer driving lessons to ensure you pass your driving test with confidence. Additionally, we provide Pass Plus schemes to further enhance your driving skills and safety on the road. Our experienced instructors provide personalized training tailored to your individual needs, covering all aspects of driving from basic skills to advanced techniques.

Find out more

Instructor Training

We offer driving instructor training on a flexible pay-as-you-go basis. Our comprehensive program covers all aspects of becoming a qualified driving instructor, including theoretical knowledge, practical driving skills, and instructional techniques. This ensures you are fully equipped to teach and guide new drivers with confidence and expertise.
